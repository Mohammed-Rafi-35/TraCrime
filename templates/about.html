About TraCrime
Our project focuses on enhancing public safety by detecting criminal activities in real-time within corridors. Leveraging advanced Machine Learning algorithms, our system identifies suspicious behavior and promptly alerts individuals in the vicinity, providing them with critical information to stay safe.



Key Features:


  • Real-time Crime Detection: Utilizes cutting-edge Machine Learning models to analyze video footage and detect criminal activities as they occur.

  • Immediate Alerts: Sends instant notifications to people near the crime scene, ensuring timely awareness and response.

  • Smart Surveillance Integration: Seamlessly integrates with existing surveillance infrastructure to enhance monitoring capabilities.

  • User-friendly Interface: Provides an intuitive dashboard for monitoring and managing alerts, accessible to both law enforcement and the general public.

  • Scalable and Adaptable: Designed to work in various environments, from small buildings to large public spaces, and can be tailored to specific security needs.



Target Audience:


  • Law enforcement agencies

  • Security personnel

  • Building management teams

  • General public concerned about safety




Our Mission


To create a safer environment by utilizing technology to detect and respond to crimes swiftly, ultimately reducing the risk and impact of criminal activities in public and private spaces.
